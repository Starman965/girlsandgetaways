Welcome to Girls and Getaways!

We're so excited to help you manage your events and groups. Here's a quick guide to get you started:

Step 1: Add People

First, head over to the Groups tab. In the People section, you can add all the fabulous ladies who will be part of your events. Just enter their full names and click Add Person.

Step 2: Create Groups

Next, still in the Groups tab, create your groups. Enter a group name and select the members from the list of people you added. Click Create Group to save your group.

Step 3: Create Events

Now, switch to the Create Event tab. Fill in the event details, select the group for the event, and choose the event type (specific date, date range, or day of the week). Add the dates and click Create Event.

Step 4: Share the Event

After creating an event, you'll get a shareable link. Copy this link and send it to your group members so they can vote on the dates. You can also open the link directly from the app.

That's it! You're all set to manage your events and groups with ease. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out. Happy planning!
