Instructions

Welcome to Girls and Getaways!

Step 1: Add People

First, head over to the Groups tab. In the People section, you can add all the fabulous ladies who will be part of any of your events. Just enter their full names and click Add Person.

Step 2: Create Groups

Next, still in the Groups tab, create your groups. Enter a group name and select the members from the list of people you added. Groups can be for your travel buddies, book club, or any other gathering. Click Create Group to save.

Step 3: Create Events

Switch to the Create Event tab and choose from three event types:

  • Specific Date: Add individual dates for each proposed meetup
  • Date Range: Select start and end dates for potential trips
  • Day of Week: Choose specific days for regular meetups

Step 4: Manage Responses

For each event you can:

  • Enable anonymous voting
  • Share the event link with your group
  • Track responses in real-time
  • Edit event details anytime
